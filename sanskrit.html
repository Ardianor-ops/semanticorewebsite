UMAP of Sanskrit Word Embeddings from FastText Clustered by HDBSCAN

To get a better understanding of the relationship between Noun Class and Lexical semantics in Sanskrit,
choose a cluster of nouns from the drop-down menu and use the tools on the side to zoom in on the dots
colored by their gender and hover over them to get the terms.

Note: Unfortunately there is no support for Sanskrit on Google Cloud Translate. This visualization was uploaded for the sake of pure transparency and to hopefully allow those proficient in the language to interpret the results.
