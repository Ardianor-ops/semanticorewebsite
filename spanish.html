UMAP of Spanish Word Embeddings from FastText Clustered by HDBSCAN

To get a better understanding of the relationship between Noun Class and Lexical semantics in Spanish,
choose a cluster of nouns from the drop-down menu and use the tools on the side to zoom in on the dots
colored by their gender and hover over them to get their translations.

Note: The nouns were translated using Google Translate and therefore there are cases of mistranslation
The encoding and clustering of the word embeddings was completely separate from this process.
